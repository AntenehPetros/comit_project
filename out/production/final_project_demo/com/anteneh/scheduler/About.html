Welcome to smart scheduling


This program is designed to generate a weekly schedule for a company.
Staff will submit their availability and will be assigned to a shift based on
seniority, availability and other additional factors.




It allows the employer to:

  • Register new employee
  • Set required staff for each day
  • Generate schedule
  • Check list of all employees

and employees to:

  • Submit availability
  • Check vacant shifts
  • Pick a shift and other
